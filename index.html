Welcome to the Passport Application Home Page!

Here, you can find information about how to apply for a passport, check the status of your application, and more.

Steps to Apply for a Passport:

  1. Gather the required documents, including proof of citizenship and identification.
  2. Complete the passport application form.
  3. Submit your application and required documents in person at a passport acceptance facility.

Additional Information:

  • Learn about passport renewal and how to update passport information.
  • Find passport acceptance facilities near you.
  • Discover expedited passport services for urgent travel needs.
  • Explore passport fees and payment options.

If you have any questions or concerns about the passport application process, please contact us. We are here to help!

Already applied for a passport? You can track the status of your application online using our Passport Application Status tool. Simply provide your last name, date of birth, and the last four digits of your Social Security number.
